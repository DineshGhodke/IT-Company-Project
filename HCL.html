HCL Technologies is a global IT services and consulting company headquartered in Noida, India. Established in 1976, it has grown to become one of the leading players in the information technology industry.
HCL operates in various domains, including IT and business process outsourcing, infrastructure services, engineering and R&D, and digital solutions. The company serves clients in diverse industries such as financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, telecommunications, and more.
Known for its customer-centric approach, HCL emphasizes the "Relationship Beyond the Contract" philosophy, aiming to build long-term partnerships with clients. The company focuses on co-innovation and collaboration to address clients' business challenges.
HCL is recognized for its commitment to social responsibility and sustainability, engaging in initiatives that contribute to education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.
